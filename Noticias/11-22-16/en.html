Important information about flights affected due to Hurricane Otto

November, 22th of 2016

For updated information about you flight(s), please visit flight status. Penalties will be waived to the flights affected as follows:

Event

Hurrican Otto

Trips to/from:

San Andres, Colombia (ADZ)

Dates

Tickets issued until:
November 22th of 2016

Original flight dates:
From November 23 to 25 of 2016

Deadline to apply for the change:
December 2nd of 2016

The new itinerary must start no later than: December 12 of 2016 according with ticket validity.

Additional Information

Affected passengers will have the option to make a change of date or flight without penalty.

The new trip must be taken not later than December 12 of 2016. Every reschedule flight after this date,will apply the respective penalties for change.

Additional travel expenses must be covered by the passenger.

Changes for origin and/or destination are not permitted.

Non refundable tickets can not be refunded. Refund fee is exempt for refundable tickets.
