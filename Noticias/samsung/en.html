Important Notification About the Samsung Galaxy Note 7 ™ Cellular Device

Panama City, Panama, Sept. 19, 2016.

Copa Airlines announces that as a result of recent incidents and concerns reported by Samsung® related to its Galaxy Note 7 cellular devices, the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) urgently advises passengers not to:

  1. Turn on, use or charge these devices on board aircraft.
  2. Store or transport these devices in checked baggage.

Safety continues to be Copa Airlines’ first priority. For that reason, we recommend that our passengers follow FAA guidelines on the use and transport of such devices aboard aircraft.
