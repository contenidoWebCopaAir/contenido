Corporate Program

Our corporate program offers special discounts on your Copa Airlines and Copa Airlines Colombia tickets while your business executives earn miles and enjoy all the advantages that ConnectMiles offers. Below you will find some of the benefits your company and your business travelers will receive by entering this personalized agreement between your company and Copa Airlines.

Benefits

  • Special discounts on published airfares.
  • ConnectMiles frequent flyer program enrollment.
  • Elite Match membership for all executives who have similar status in other frequent flyer programs.
  • Check-in at customer service ConnectMiles PreferMember lines.
  • Business Class upgrade at the airport, subject to availability.**

** Applies to tickets with Y fare within one year of issuance.

For more information, contact us at corporate@copaair.com
